The University

Highly competetive university that is recognised globally, being number 50th and above in most world rankings for CS.

One of the 8 original red brick universities in the UK, and a part of the Russel Group Universities. It has incredible engineering facilities, and a dedicated building for computer science students.

Visit the university website

Facilities

The University of Manchester has a multitude of facilities for all the students that study here. Ranging from dedicated buildings for each branch of the university to multiple 24/7 libraries and study areas.

Research

Have a look at the N8 Research Partnership website. Find research facilities that can take your organisation to the next level.

The University Library

The University of Manchester Library is one of five national research libraries.

Accomodations

The accommodations offer a variety of guest rooms and one-bedroom suites. They are well connected to local and international transport hubs, including Manchester Piccadilly Train Station and Manchester Airport.

The Campus

There are 3 campuses in the University of Manchester, North Campus - Sackville region, Central Campus - Oxford Road, and South Campus - Fallowfield With a variety of ameneties such as sports centers, pubs and convenience stores inbetween the different areas.

14 Computer Clusters

The benefit of having so many computer clusters is that it allows for students and staff to work on their tasks nearly anywhere across the entire campus.

Student Support

Providing aid and help to students in need, when they need it. Regardless of any issue, there is almost always someone or something to help you with it.

Student Union

Student Union gives an opportunity for student to be heard, make new friends and join societies. More info available in our Student Life section
